Verbs can also be classified as either
finite or non-finite.

A finite verb can stand by itself as the main verb of a sentence.
  • The car smashed the restaurant.
  • The leaves were yellow and brown.
  • Where am I?
Non-finite verbs cannot, by themselves, be main verbs:
  • I was waiting for the bus.
  • I am going to the movies.
  • I have been ill.
